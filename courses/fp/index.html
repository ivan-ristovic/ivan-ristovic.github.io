[FP 4I] Functional Programming / Funkcionalno programiranje

Course information

Professor: Ivan Čukić
Assistents: Ivan Ristović & Stefan Kapunac

The final exam is not divisible, i.e. parts of it cannot be reused. Theoretical questions and practice tasks are taken at the same time.
Practice tasks: 60 points
Theoretical questions: 40 points

Requirement for passing the final exam: have atleast 30% of points on every exam part (practice, theory)

Literature (for practices):

  • Haskell WikiBook
  • M. Lipovača: Learn You a Haskell for Great Good!
  • B. O'Sullivan, D. Stewart, and J. Goerzen: Real world Haskell
  • I. Čukić: Functional Programming in C++
